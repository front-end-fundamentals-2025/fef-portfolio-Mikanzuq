Dripping metal words - it's a graphic design showing metal covered words melt

Inspiration behind this art was Game of Thrones scene with Khal Drogo pouring melt gold over Viserys head, a "golden crown".

I used After Effects to make this design. To finish this look I used a lot of filters and layers. After everything I rendered it and by using capcut app i added a raining sound to make it feel even more 3D.
